Introducing the Medlock Hotel.

Where contemporary style meets true Manchester warmth.

Set in the heart of the Entertainment District, we blend modern design with an inviting, down-to-earth feel that makes every stay effortless.

Whether you’re here for business, football, music, or a weekend away, our spaces are designed for comfort, calm, and connection.

Our Rooms

From our Standard Rooms all the way up to the Pitchview and Penthouse Suites, every space offers a calm retreat from the energy of the city.

Clean, modern design, warm finishes, and thoughtful details create a stay that feels relaxed, refreshed, and distinctly Manchester.

The Brasserie

Experience the best of local flavors at The Brasserie, our signature dining space. From hearty breakfasts to elegant evening meals, we serve up classic dishes with a modern Manchester twist.

With an inviting atmosphere and a menu focused on seasonal ingredients, it's the perfect spot for a relaxed meal or a special celebration.

Wellness & Gym

Keep up your routine in our state-of-the-art Gym and Wellness suite. Equipped with the latest fitness technology, it's designed to help you stay energized during your stay.

After your workout, unwind in our tranquil wellness spaces, ensuring you leave feeling refreshed and ready for whatever the city has in store.

Spaces made for big ideas

From focused start-up sessions to creative team meet-ups, our Pitchview Rooms offer flexible, light-filled spaces designed to help ideas flow. For something a little more celebratory, our two-floor Penthouse sets the scene perfectly — a standout venue for private dinners, milestone moments, and events that deserve a city-skyline backdrop.

With warm service and a central location, hosting at The Medlock feels effortless from start to finish.

Our rooms

Where contemporary style meets true Manchester warmth.

Set in the heart of the Entertainment District, we blend modern design with an inviting, down-to-earth feel that makes every stay effortless.

Standard Rooms

Your Modern Manchester Base.

Sleek lines, soft lighting and warm, natural textures create an inviting atmosphere for both business and leisure travellers.

Enjoy a comfortable king or twin bed, smart TV, walk-in shower, and a space designed for clarity and focus. Perfect for: short stays, concert goers, business trips or a base for game day.

Pitchview Room

The game-changing experience.

Bright, modern spaces with sweeping views over the stadium, bringing a touch of energy and excitement to your stay.

Clean design, warm finishes, and thoughtful comforts make these rooms perfect for guests who want a little more inspiration from their surroundings.

Pitchview Rooms give you a calm place to unwind with a front-row view of the city's sporting spirit.

Premium Room

Premium made perfect.

Spacious, bright, and designed for comfort, our suites are the perfect city retreat.

Each suite features a generous open-plan layout, including a separate living area ideal for relaxing or entertaining, and is filled with natural daylight that brings a warm, welcoming atmosphere to every corner.

Thoughtful touches and modern design make every stay feel effortless, whether you're here for work, leisure, or a little of both.

Penthouse Suite

Your City Escape, Elevated.

Experience Manchester from above in our spectacular two-story Penthouse.

With sweeping views over Co-op Live and the city centre, this expansive space combines luxury, style, and comfort across two levels.

A separate living area, dining space, and private terrace create the perfect setting for entertaining or relaxing, while large windows flood the Penthouse with natural light, capturing the energy and spirit of the city below.

Designed for special occasions, extended stays, or guests who simply want the very best, the Penthouse is Manchester living at its finest.
